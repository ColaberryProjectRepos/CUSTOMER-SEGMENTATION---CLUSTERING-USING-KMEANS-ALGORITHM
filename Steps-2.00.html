you will find dataset in:  customer_segmentation\input\Mall_customers.csv

Domain: Retail Industry

Size of the dataset: 200 rows, 5 columns

·      Dataset contains 200 membership card details of the customers taken from a supermarket.

·       It contains basic information like (ID, age, gender, income, spending score).

ATTRIBUTE

DESCRIPTION

Customer_ID 

Unique ID assigned to the customer

Gender

Gender of the customer 

Age

Age of the customer 

Annual_Income(k$)

Annual Income of the customer

Spending_Score 

Score assigned based on spending nature (1 to 100)
